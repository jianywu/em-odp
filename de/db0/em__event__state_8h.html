◆ FREE_CNT_INIT

#define FREE_CNT_INIT   ((uint16_t)0x0100) /* = 0 + 'offset' */

Init values for the event-state counters 'free_cnt' and 'send_cnt'.

The counters are 32-bit but are updated as one combined 64-bit atomic var, thus the init values are in the middle of the u32-range to avoid wraparounds when decrementing below '0'.
